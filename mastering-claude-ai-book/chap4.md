# Chapter 4: The Art of Prompting: Getting Better Responses

## The Anatomy of a Perfect Prompt

- context
- specificity
- constraints
- examples
- format

## Advanced Prompting Techniques

- Role-playing
- Chain-of-thought prompting
- Few-shot prompting
- Zero-shot prompting
- Self-consistency prompting
- Iterative prompting
    - First Attempt
    - Analyze
    - Refine
    - Repeat


## Pitfalls to Avoid

- The Perfect Prompt Fallacy
- One Size Fits All
- Overloading the Prompt
- Over-constrainting


## The Prompt Engineering Checklist

- Have I provided enough context?
- Is my prompt specific enough?
- Did I specify the format I want?
- Are my constraints reasonable and compatible?
- Would examples help clarfiy my need?
- Am I asking for one thing at a time?
- Do I have a backup approach if this doesn't work?


## Conclusion

Prompt engineering is about clear communication, thoughtful structure and iterative refinement.
